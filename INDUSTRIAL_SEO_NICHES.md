# Niche Industrial E-Commerce — SEO-Driven Reselling

Research compiled 2026-05-12. Target profile: solo technical founder, ~$1K capital, online-only, Next.js + SEO, no manufacturer relationships at start, distributor sourcing only.

The pattern that actually works: **cross-reference data + part-number landing pages = the entire SEO moat.** No content marketing, no ads. Just product pages ranking on specific part-number searches by buyers in pain (broken HVAC at 2am, broken oven at 11pm).

## Anti-patterns — avoid these

- **China direct-import** — Section 321 de minimis suspended Aug 29, 2025. 120% ad-valorem duty or $200/parcel flat fee on most generic Chinese industrial goods.
- **MAP-policed brands** (Pentair, Hayward, Belimo retail-policed SKUs, Honeywell HVAC retail) — margins compressed to 10-18%
- **Generic categories** — bearings, generic o-rings, generic hand tools — Grainger/Zoro/McMaster/Amazon Industrial own these SERPs
- **Auto parts (general)** — RockAuto, eBay Motors, AutoZone own these
- **Anything requiring fit/feel** — premium hand tools, ergonomic equipment
- **Aerospace / mil-spec** — AS9100 paperwork barrier
- **HazMat-shipping items** — chemicals, refrigerants (EPA 608), pesticides, paint
- **Locksmith automotive** — state licensing required

## The 60-minute validation experiment

Before any LLC, before any code, run this for your chosen niche:

1. Pick **5 specific part numbers** in the niche
2. Google each one
3. Check page 1: **are the top results weak?** (Old catalogs, ugly PHP pages, no rich snippets, no image carousels)
4. **If 3 of 5 SERPs look beatable by a clean Next.js page** with JSON-LD Product schema + dimensional drawings + cross-ref tables → real opportunity
5. If most show big consolidators on page 1 → harder; try long-tail variants (`[part] manual`, `[part] wiring diagram`)

This tells you in 60 minutes whether the niche is winnable for you.

## The 90-day launch plan (HVAC controls example)

**Week 1 (~$150):**
- Form single-member LLC + EIN + state resale cert
- Apply to HVAC USA reseller program (also Wholesale Controls International, ACI as backup)
- Buy domain ($12)

**Week 2-3 ($0):**
- Build skeleton Next.js + Vercel storefront
- Reverse-engineer 5 competitor product-page structures
- Hand-write 20 part-number pages: technical specs, wiring diagrams, cross-references, FAQ, JSON-LD Product schema

**Week 4 ($0):**
- Submit sitemap to Google Search Console
- Build the next 30 part-number pages
- Helpful technical answers on r/HVAC, ControlTalk, HVAC-Talk forum (be useful, no spam)

**Month 2-3:**
- Watch which pages start ranking
- Double down on what works
- First sale should hit Month 3 if traffic compounds

**Trajectory (estimated, not measured):**
- Month 1-2: $0
- Month 3: $300-$800
- Month 6: $3K-$8K
- Month 12: $15K-$40K
- Month 18: $40K-$80K plausible
